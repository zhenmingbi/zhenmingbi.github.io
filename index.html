Research Interests

My research area is graph theory. In my dissertation, I study graph structures, Hamiltonian graphs and Hamiltonian-related graph properties in graphs. I am also interested in graph colorings, graph decompositions, Ramsey numbers, distance in graphs as well as applied graph theory.

My Erdős Number is 2 (collaborators: Ping Zhang and Gary Chartrand). Publications in MathSciNet.
